New Island! IO Vs The Seven

Posted: 29/10/23
Hello All! Today, I thought I would share my most recent FCHQ Feature Friday (week 210) winning map, The Island: IO Vs The Seven! In my opinion, I think this is probably the best non-racing map I have made, and maybe even the best map I have ever made entirely.

So what is this map then? Well, its a 2-16 player 3 phase gamemode where 2 teams (The IO & The Seven) fight! The IO must destroy The Seven's base whilst The Seven must try to stop them!

The inspiration
I love Star Wars Battlefront II (2017)'s Galactic Assault mode and that is where the initial idea for this project came from. Some kind of infiltrating team must be stopped by a defending team, and it evolved from there really into this.

Closing thoughts
When I originally published this island, it was made in Fortnite Creative 1.0. After receiving some feedback, I decided to convert it into a UEFN project and continue development on there. The map has changed a lot since launch, mainly with the focus of easing the learning experience for new players. I recommend giving this one a play with a few friends.

Thanks for reading!
