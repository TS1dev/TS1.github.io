Late Game: Titled Towers

Posted: 13/04/24
Hello All! Today, I thought I would share my most recent map, Late Game: Titled Towers! This is my first attempt at making a competetive-style map (other than Red Vs Blue maps).

Very simply it is a Battle Royale simulation that takes place souly in Tilted Towers.

Closing thoughts
This project was built in UEFN and is my first real attempt at using Verse in a map. There is loads of Verse packed into this map! It took over 20 versions of this map to get it all working. Why not play with some friends?
